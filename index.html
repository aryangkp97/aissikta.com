Admission Procedure

Admissions are made on the basis of an entrance examination. The principal reserves the right to admit a child into the class in which the child is found fit for, after examining the child.

Transportaion

Transportation is available for the children.

  • Preference will be given to small children first.
  • Transportation fee will be charged for twelve months, it will not be refunded.
  • Bus will run on the prescribed routes only.

Payment of Fees

The total fees for the year should be paid in cash on three month basis. The parents shall deposit the first installment of the fees at the time of admission.

Entrance Exam

The entrance examination will be conducted at Aryan International School. The medium of examination will be English. The date of examination will be communicated at the time of registration.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original + Copy)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school (if applicable)
  • Character Certificate
  • Medical Report
  • Passport size photographs (4-6 nos.)
  • Proof of residence

Scholarship & Awards

Aryan International School offers merit-based scholarships to deserving students. Students with exceptional academic performance and extracurricular achievements are eligible for scholarship programs.
